Vegan Kimchi

Description

Kimchi is a staple in the Korea cuisine. This is a vegan version.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium nappa cabbage (about 800g)
  • 2 medium carrots
  • Asian radish
  • 2 green onions
  • 1.5 cups of water (360 mL)
  • 1/2 onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 3 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 kombu seaweed
  • 1 piece of ginger
  • 1/2 cup of gochugaru
  • 3/4 cup of coarse salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of glutinous rice flour

Steps

  1. Cut your cabbage in 2 lengthwise, salt between each leaf at the level of the heart, and immerse your cabbage for 12 hours in brine (slightly salted warm water).
  2. Once the cabbage is well washed and drained, spread a layer of kimchi paste between each leaf and let it ferment in an airtight container.
