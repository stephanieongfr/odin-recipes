Healthy Moist Banana Bread

Description

Ultra moist banana bread... This recipe is healthy and vegan!

Ingredients

  • 3 medium ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup (80g) reduced aquafaba
  • 1 cup (120g) Greek-style soy yogurt, plain and unsweetened
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups (240g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup (80g) brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup (80g) chocolate chips
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix all the dry ingredients together.
  2. In another bowl, mash the bananas then add all the wet ingredients except the aquafaba.
  3. Preheat the oven at 360°F (180°C).
  4. Whip the aquafaba.
  5. Gently fold the aquafaba into the wet ingredients, then the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.
  6. Add the chocolate chips – do not overmix.
  7. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in the oven for 50 min at 360°F (180°C).
  8. Wait until the banana bread cools down a bit before removing the loaf from the pan and let it cool down completely on a rack.
